WELCOME

Hi there, I'm glad you could make it.
My name is Kwinten, I am a creative and venturous spirit.
Having been fascinated with technology and its limitless potential my whole life, I decided to take it to the next level when enduring in a 7 month developer bootcamp that started in december 2018.

Before this I had already taught myself the building blocks of the web: HTML, CSS and JavaScript. During my time at school I was able to get an even bigger taste of everything web development has to offer. I was introduced to: Git, jQuery, Bootstrap PHP, MAMP, React, API, NPM, Node.js, Express.js, and WordPress.

At the same time I started to get applications for real projects and was able to make great steps towards my goal of making an impact in the digital world. After the bootcamp I endured in a internship at Radikal. A digital agency with a main focus on web development.
